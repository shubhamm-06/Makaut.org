MAKAUT 2024-2025 Academic Calender

Download PDF

With a new academic year on the horizon, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) in West Bengal has released its academic calendar for 2024-25. This calendar is a vital tool for students, faculty, and staff, providing a clear schedule to ensure a smooth and successful academic journey. Let’s take a closer look at what the year ahead holds.

Odd Semester 2024-25: Kicking Off the Year

The academic year begins with the odd semesters for students in the 3rd, 5th, 7th, and 9th semesters. Start of Academic Programs: Classes for these semesters begin on July 15, 2024. Enrollment: Students must complete their enrollment from July 22, 2024, to July 31, 2024. New Students: First-semester students will begin as per the admission schedule, bringing fresh faces and energy to the campus.

Continuous Assessments and Pre-Examination Activities

To maintain a steady pace of learning, several continuous assessment (CA) submissions are scheduled: CA1 Submission: August 12-16, 2024 CA2 & PCA1 Submission: September 17-20, 2024 CA3 Submission: October 21-24, 2024 CA4 & PCA2 Submission: November 11-16, 2024 Pre-Examination Activities: November 18-25, 2024

Examination Time

The culmination of the odd semester includes a comprehensive exam schedule: Theory Examinations: November 28 - December 14, 2024 Practical, Sessional, and Viva-Voce Examinations: December 16-21, 2024

Even Semester 2024-25: Continuing the Journey

After a brief winter break, the even semesters begin, including the 2nd, 4th, 6th, 8th, and 10th semesters. Start of Academic Programs: January 6, 2025 Enrollment: January 10-17, 2025

Keeping the Momentum: Continuous Assessments and Pre-Examination Activities

The even semester maintains the rhythm of regular assessments and preparations: CA1 Submission: February 3-7, 2025 CA2 & PCA1 Submission: March 3-7, 2025 CA3 Submission: April 3-7, 2025 CA4 & PCA2 Submission: May 5-10, 2025 Pre-Examination Activities: May 13-20, 2025

Final Push: Examination Schedule

The end-of-semester exams for even semesters are scheduled as follows: Theory Examinations: May 23 - June 10, 2025 Practical, Sessional, and Viva-Voce Examinations: June 16-21, 2025

Special Dates for MAR and MOOCs

To ensure comprehensive learning, MAKAUT integrates MAR (Mandatory Additional Requirement) and MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses): MAR Upload for Odd Semester: August 1 - December 31, 2024 MAR Upload for Even Semester: February 1 - May 31, 2025 MOOCs Upload and Verification: August 1, 2024 - May 31, 2025 MOOCs Credit Transfer: To be completed by the end of the semester exams

Results Timeline

Timely results are crucial for students’ academic progression: Odd Semester Results: By April 30, 2025 Even Semester Results: By October 31, 2025

Flexibility and Adaptability

The dates provided are well thought out, but they are tentative and may be adjusted as needed. This flexibility ensures that the university can adapt to any unexpected situations without compromising academic quality.

Conclusion

MAKAUT's academic calendar for 2024-25 is a well-structured roadmap that ensures a balanced and comprehensive academic experience. By adhering to this schedule, students and faculty can efficiently navigate through the academic year, ensuring timely progress and success. As we move forward, this calendar will be a pivotal tool in achieving educational excellence and fostering a productive academic environment.

Note: For a detailed Academic Calender for the year 2024-2025, you can refer to the PDF provided by MAKAUT University on their official website.
