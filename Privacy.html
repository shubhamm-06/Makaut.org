Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20th July 2023

Thank you for visiting Makaut.org! We are committed to maintaining the privacy and protection of your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide to us through our "Contact Me" form.

When you use the "Contact Me" form on Makaut.org, we may collect the following information:

  • Name: We use this information to address you properly when responding to your message.
  • Email Address: We collect your email address to reply to your inquiries and provide any necessary information.
Data Handling

  • We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties without your explicit consent, except when required by law.
  • Your data will be stored securely and only retained for as long as necessary to address your inquiry or as required by law.
Cookies and Analytics

Makaut.org may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies do not collect personal information and are used for statistical purposes only.

Third-Party Services

Makaut.org may contain links to third-party websites or services. However, we do not have control over their privacy practices, and this privacy policy applies only to Makaut.org. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as needed to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.

Your Consent

By using the Contact Me form on Makaut.org, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this privacy policy.
